Gameracy is a tabletop gaming programme that helps young people to improve their literacy, numeracy, teamwork and strategisation skills, all while having lots of fun.

Benefits of Gaming

Gaming and literacy go hand-in-hand; games come with instructions, menus and more. Learning the language and mechanics of any game involves acquiring a new vocabulary.

Gaming encourages positive group participation skills.

Tabletop Games develop social and recreational skills.

Gaming is an enrichment activity that supports wellbeing.

Tabletop games can become a lifelong, enjoyable hobby.

Gaming allows players to express ideas and make creative choices

Tabletop games develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
